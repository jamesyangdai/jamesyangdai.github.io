Del Norte Web Development Club

Founded with the purpose of combining web development, education, and helping others, Del Norte High School Web Development Club serves to design and publish high quality websites and webpages.

  • About Us

Founders

Founded in the fall of 2017 by students James Dai and Andrew Liu, Del Norte Web Development Club was created with the sole purpose of combining the power of the internet with education. The two founders along with numerous friends and club members would go on to establish the student run organization.

  • Leadership

Projects

With the goal in mind of helping others, Del Norte Web Development Club focuses a majority of its time by building websites and webpages for others. Whether a website is built for a peer organization or school project, all of our work can be found here.

Core Principles

We like to believe that we're more than just another club. Through our core principles, we're able to demonstrate our unique skills that help make us successful.

Student Led

Del Norte Web Development Club is 100% student led along with all projects created by club members.

STEM Focused

With a heavy focus on technology, our projects are all created through HTML and CSS.

Teamwork

Collaboration is key as all members have an equally important role in everything we do.
